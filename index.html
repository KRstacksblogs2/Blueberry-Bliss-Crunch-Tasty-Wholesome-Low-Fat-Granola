What’s Inside Every Bag

With each bag, you're getting a handful of wholesome ingredients that work together to elevate your snack or breakfast:

  • Certified gluten-free oats – The hearty base that provides lasting energy and satisfying crunch.
  • Organic virgin coconut oil – A clean, plant-based fat source that supports heart health and helps deliver that perfect toasted texture.
  • Organic whole golden flaxseed – A powerhouse of omega-3s and fiber to support digestion and overall wellness.
  • Organic blue agave nectar – A naturally low-glycemic sweetener that adds just the right touch of sweetness.
  • Cultivated blueberries – Bursting with natural antioxidants, these chewy gems bring tart-sweet balance to every bite.
  • Pure vanilla and natural orange extracts – For a warm, aromatic flavor profile that enhances the fruit and oat blend.
  • A hint of kosher salt and golden brown sugar – These round out the flavor, bringing out both the sweetness and the complexity in each bite.

Every ingredient is selected with intention: no fillers, no artificial additives, just clean, premium components that support flavor, texture, and a better-for-you approach to snacking.
